Como in Adventure

Help Como to bring back his kidnapped sisters!

Dr.Skat and his fellow kittens made their sneaky plan to kidnap Pios for their fine dinner. Now, it’s your time to join Como’s epic journey and rescue his beloved sisters!

SIMPLE AND EASY CONTROL
Jump and run are two essentials for side-scroller, but that’s not all indeed! Try more than 10 different actions performable using two buttons

CHALLENGING STAGES
77 stages full of tricks and traps. Try to collect all stars by clearing more than 200 achievements.

AMAZING WORLD THEMES
From grassy land to scorching desert and sandy beach to simmering volcanic land. Your discovery is the progress to the next step. Go for it now!

FUN MODES
Try all 5 different modes ranging from saving Pios to racing the clock. Epic boss battle at the end of each zone!

SKATS AND SISTERS
Skats are everywhere prowling for Pio. Beat them up before they snatch her from Como!

  • Android: Google Play
  • Category: Mobile Game

Hungry Mates

Eat a lot, survive long and score high!

“Easy, but definitely challenging”

The creator of Como in Adventure has teamed up with two other talented developers and come back with Hungry Mates, the simplest but most addictive casual game you will find hard to put down.

Who left the penguin stuck in a hole?
Why the mole wearing THAT blue knit cap?
What is really on the jungle monkey’s head?

Curious? Play and find out yourself! Feed the hungry animals and make your own collection of delicious food!

FEATURES
- Simple rules: Eat a lot, survive long and score high!
- Easy control: Just tap left/right to feed or dodge
- Collect dozens of food themes and unlock new characters
- Challenge your friends! Beat the world ranking!
- Free to play, NO annoying ads, NO internet required

  • Date: 2015-08-26
  • iOS: App Store
  • Android: Google Play
  • Category: Mobile Game

Snake VR

Do not just trap your snake in a small confined screen, now it can freely move around 360 degrees.
